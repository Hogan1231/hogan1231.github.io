About Me

I am a second-year MA student in Translation Studies at the Graduate Institute of Interpretation and Translation (GIIT), Shanghai International Studies University, under the supervision of Associate Professor Zhang Ying. I expect to graduate in June 2027.

My research focuses on translation history in the late Qing and early Republican China, with particular emphasis on Chinese opera translation and translator networks. I employ digital humanities methodologies, including social network analysis and corpus-based approaches, to examine historical translation practices and cross-cultural transmission patterns.

Research Interests

  • Translation history in the late Qing and early Republican China
  • Network analysis and digital methods in translation studies
  • Translation of Chinese opera in the West
