History, Statistics, Network: Mapping the Translation of Detective Fiction in Late Qing and Early Republican China
Foreign Studies《外文研究》, accepted for publication, forthcoming. (Single author)

Applied social network analysis using Gephi to map the network of detective fiction translation activities in late Qing and early Republican China. Combined quantitative visualization with historical interpretation.

Translation Studies from a Critical Race Perspective: A Review of Translation and Race
Asia Pacific Interdisciplinary Translation Studies《亞太跨學科翻譯研究》, Vol.18, 2027, forthcoming. (First author)

Book review of Corine Tachtiris's Translation and Race, bridging Critical Race Theory with translation studies and proposing a research agenda for integrating racial discourse into Chinese translation scholarship.
